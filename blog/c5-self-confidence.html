Self Awareness

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

They are essentially the same. They are both primarily about self-reflection, being aware of your thoughts, sensations and emotions, and both have the end result of improving your emotional intelligence.

Try at least one of the self awareness practices from the chapter (Body Scan, Journaling). What was your experience of it? How did you feel?

I did a body scan, and found it a pleasent experience. I have done these in the past, but from my toes to my head, so the SIY version was an interesting contrast. Remember a positive experience and experiencing it's effects on my body was very bizarre. Throughout the first phase of the exercise I was very tense and stressed, but once I started thinking about the positive experience I could feel the changes in my body as I become more relaxed and more aware.

What did you take away from the "My Emotions are Not Me" section?

That I need to remember this, and stop letting my emotions overwhelm me. The cloud imagery seems like it will be useful, as if I imagine my emotions as clouds they should (theoretically) be able to float away just as easily.
