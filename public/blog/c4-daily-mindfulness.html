Daily Mindfulness

What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

It is a good way of approaching conversation, but we both found it a bit much to formally enforce it. Giving someone your full attention when they are speaking seems less a matter of 'mindfulness' and more a matter of politeness.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Number 2, where you do less than you can and meditate for less time than it takes for me to get bored. For me this means I will meditate for <5 mins at a time, at least once a day.

Any other musings?

I found the concept of walking as a form of meditation interesting. Particularly when paired with the 'Joyful Meditation'idea. I will be sure to practise this as often as possible, particularly with the weather getting warmer and the beach a short walk down the road.
